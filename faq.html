What is CKD?

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a condition in which the kidneys lose their function over time. It can lead to kidney failure if not managed properly.

How can I predict CKD?

You can predict the likelihood of CKD by providing medical details such as age, blood pressure, albumin levels, and other health parameters using our website's prediction tool.
